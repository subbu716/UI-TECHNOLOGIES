Welcome to Government Polytechnic, Kamalapuram

MISSION:

Our efforts are dedicated to impart quality and value based education to meet the needs of profession and society. Our strength is directed to develop talented human resource having innovation, creativity, team-spirit and entrepreneurial leadership. Our endeavour is to provide excellence in teaching-learning for promoting research and development activities to serve as a valuable resource for industry and society.

VISION:

To facilitate transformation of students into good human beings, responsible citizens and competent professionals. To achieve excellent standards of quality education with rapidly changing technologies and create technical manpower of global standards with capabilities of accepting new challenges. To practice and promote high standards of professional ethics, transparency and accountability.
